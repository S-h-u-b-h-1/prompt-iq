Privacy Policy for PromptIQ

Last updated: June 29, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how PromptIQ collects, uses, and shares your data when you use the PromptIQ Chrome Extension and associated services.

1. What Data We Collect

To provide prompt optimization and scoring, PromptIQ collects the following data types:

  • Prompt Text (Personal Communications): We collect the text of the prompt you choose to optimize in order to send it to the Gemini API (via our secure serverless backend) to perform the optimization.
  • Website Content: We read the active chatbox input field content locally on supported AI platforms (e.g. ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini) solely to calculate the prompt quality score (0-100) dynamically as you type.
  • User Activity Metadata: We collect metadata about optimization events (such as the AI platform name, the original score, the optimized score, and the time of the event) to populate your history logs and dashboard statistics.
  • Account Information: If you create an account, we collect your email address and password authentication data. Passwords are stored only as salted hashes.
  • Subscription Metadata: If you upgrade to Pro, Stripe processes your payment and we store subscription status, plan, and Stripe customer/subscription identifiers.
  • Survey Responses: If you answer optional in-product research questions, we store those responses to improve PromptIQ and understand customer needs.
  • Website Analytics: Our website may use Google Analytics and externally hosted assets such as Google Fonts to understand site usage and render the page.

2. How Data Is Stored

  • Local Storage: Your configuration settings (tier preferences) and a local cache of your history are stored on your device using IndexedDB and Chrome Storage Local APIs.
  • Database Storage: A copy of your optimized prompts, scores, and installation ID is transmitted to and stored securely on our central Neon Postgres database via our Vercel serverless backend.

3. How Data Is Used

We use the collected data solely for the following purposes:

  • To calculate your prompt scores in real-time.
  • To perform prompt optimizations using the Google Gemini API.
  • To save and synchronize your optimization runs history so you can retrieve them in your extension popup.
  • To monitor general system performance and improve the prompt engineering models.

4. Data Sharing & Third Parties

  • No Sale of Data: We do not sell, rent, or trade your data to third parties.
  • Third-Party APIs: Prompt optimizations are processed using the Google Gemini API. The prompt text, selected AI platform, optimization mode, and related instructions are shared with Google's API to complete the request. We do not include your account email or password in these requests.
  • Payments: Paid subscriptions are processed by Stripe. PromptIQ does not store full card numbers.
  • Website Services: Google Analytics, Google Fonts, and CDN-hosted frontend assets may receive standard browser request metadata when you visit PromptIQ web pages.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

We retain your optimization history in our database so you can review past prompt improvements. You can delete all history records associated with your account or installation ID at any time by clicking the "Clear All" button in the History tab of the PromptIQ popup interface. This action wipes the data both locally and from our central server. Account, subscription, survey, and operational logs may be retained as needed to operate the service, handle billing, prevent abuse, and meet legal obligations.

6. Policy Certifications

  • We do not sell or transfer user data to third parties outside of the approved Gemini API use case.
  • We do not use or transfer user data for purposes unrelated to PromptIQ's single core function.
  • We do not use or transfer user data to determine creditworthiness or for lending purposes.
