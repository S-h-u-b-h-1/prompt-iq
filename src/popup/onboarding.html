SEC

Secure and transparent by design

Data handling is limited to providing prompt optimization, account access, history, feedback, and subscriptions.

  • Free optimization and signed-out history remain on this device.
  • Signed-in history is synchronized to your PromptIQ account.
  • Premium sends prompts you choose to optimize to the PromptIQ backend and Google Gemini.
  • Stripe processes Premium payments. PromptIQ does not store full card numbers.
  • Prompt data is not sold or used for personalized advertising.
